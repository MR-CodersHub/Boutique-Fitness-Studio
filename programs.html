Focus on Alignment

The Reformer Pilates Method

Our reformer Pilates methodology is rooted in absolute physical control. Using premium spring-loaded reformer machines, we guide you through sequences that decompress the spine, strengthen the transverse abdominis, and target hard-to-reach stabilizing muscles. It is the gold standard for creating lean definition and supporting orthopedic longevity.

Focus on Mindset

Mindful Vinyasa & Meditation

VYORA Yoga Fusion is a combination of joint stability drills and breath-led flows. We transition between isometric strength holds and open-heart mobility stretches, followed by a 10-minute sound bath meditation using crystal singing bowls. It is designed to lower autonomic anxiety and return your body to a rested state.

Cellular Hydration

Organic Botanical Nutrition & Tea

At VYORA, we believe that fitness transitions seamlessly into the fuel you feed your body at a cellular level. We have collaborated with certified macro-botanists to curate seasonal organic tea infusions available complimentary at our post-workout lounge.

Whether it's our *Ginger-Ginseng Warm Infusion* designed to stimulate circulation and metabolic thermogenesis before class, or our *Chilled Lavender-Hibiscus Cleanse* to flush away structural lactic acid post-workout, we ensure your recovery starts the second you step off the reformer mat.
