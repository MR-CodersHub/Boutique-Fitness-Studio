First-Time FAQ & Guide

Everything you need to know before stepping inside our luxury boutique wellness sanctuary.

Please wear comfortable, form-fitting activewear that allows you to move freely. For **Precision Pilates Reformer** sessions, **non-slip grip socks** are mandatory for safety and hygiene. If you do not have a pair, premium grip socks are available for purchase at our concierge desk. We provide organic luxury towels, mats, and structured cellular hydration, so you do not need to bring yours.
We highly recommend first-time members arrive **15 minutes early**. This gives you ample time to check in, tour the luxury changing rooms, meet your certified master instructor, adjust the reformer spring settings, and receive custom posture or alignment assessment modifications. Please note that for safety and focus, late entries are not permitted once a session has commenced.
Since VYORA classes are capped strictly at **12 members** to maintain boutique customization, we operate a **12-hour cancellation policy**. If you cancel your booking more than 12 hours in advance, your class credit will be fully restored. Cancellations made within the 12-hour window are forfeit. If a class is full, you can join the waitlist; our automated system will notify you immediately via text/email once a slot opens.
